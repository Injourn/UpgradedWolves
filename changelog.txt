v1.1.0

Added:
+Personalities, expressions, and behavior
Personalities are natural traits given to wolves and are applied randomly. 
All Powerup behaviors will take precedent over personality behaviors.

+Personalities
	+ Aggressive Personality
	+ Passive personality.
	+ Social Personality
	+ Dominant Personality
	+ Affectionate Personality
	+ Playful Personality

+ Expressions
	+ Aggressive Follow Expression
	+ Socialize Expression
	+ Playful expression.
	+ intimidate expression
	+ Passive expression

Behaviors act like sub personalities, and influence the main personality of a wolf.
